Backend Development with Node.js

Overview

Learn the art of building efficient and scalable server-side applications with Node.js in our comprehensive Backend Development course in Bangalore, India. This training provides hands-on experience with the fundamentals of Node.js and its ecosystem, enabling you to create APIs and server-side applications.

The course covers advanced topics such as RESTful API design, Express.js framework, database integration with MongoDB, and authentication mechanisms. You will also gain exposure to debugging, testing, and deploying backend solutions to the cloud.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, this course offers in-depth knowledge and practical skills that prepare you for a career as a professional backend developer.
